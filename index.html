What's My Story?

I have been working in engineering or tech roles and have been programming something for as long as I can remember: be it automotive or marine electrical systems, industrial robots and controls, hmi's, IoT things, cad scripts, simple static web pages, web-based time-saving tools, and even responsive full-stack apps complete with databases and api's.

I have been focused on cad, robotics, and software engineering professionally since 2004 and have had the opportunity to work on some amazingly fun and intriguing projects for some really great companies and people.

I'm passionate about the creative process and making things: be it a physical object, it's digital twin, or writing the code to make it work, and work well.

My goal and passion is always to create an efficient and intuitive software experience for the end user. I love automating the boring stuff (Thanks Al!) and improving or streamlining the tried and true.

But even when I'm not programming, I'm trying to think of a more efficient ways to do whatever it is I'm doing.

And on the flip-side of that, sometimes it's necessary to drag out the process a little while longer: like fermenting a loaf of sourdough in the fridge overnight to bake the next morning, or carefully analyzing a system to ask the right questions for redesign.

I've worked in many industries with a lot of great people and they've all taught me a thing or two (and so much more!). I can't wait to join my next team to learn and grow while making something great, or something great again.
